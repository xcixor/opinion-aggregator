Aim

Our idea is to create an online system that can help the governments in united Arab emirates to study the new generation and study the personality types of young people to understand their attitudes, ideas, and beliefs, skills. The result of this project can help the government to know what the new generation is good at, what it likes, what it wants to be in the future, jobs its likely to engage in and its dislikes.

The main idea of this project is to help the government predict how the next generations will be and what jobs will be in demand and which ones will not. This can help the governments to plan for, and implement policies that will be beneficial to this generation.
